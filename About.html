OUR STORY

We are a student-led journal focused on Finance, Economics, and Accounting. What started as shared curiosity has grown into a platform for analysis, commentary, and reporting. ​

Our goal is to bring fresh student perspectives on financial and economic issues—making complex ideas clear, relevant, and thought-provoking. ​

Join us as we document and question the world of finance and economics, one article at a time.
